The opening line of The Brothers Karamazov by Fyodor Dostoyevsky

Alexei Fyodorovich Karamazov was the third son of a landowner from our district, Fyodor Pavlovich Karamazov, well known in his own day (and still remembered among us) because of his dark and tragic death, which happened exactly thirteen years ago and I which I shall speak of in its proper place.

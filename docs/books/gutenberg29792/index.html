The opening line of Buffalo Bill's Spy Trailer; Or, The Stranger in Camp by Prentiss Ingraham

A horseman drew rein one morning, upon the brink of the Grand Cañon of the Colorado, a mighty abyss, too vast for the eye to take in its grand immensity; a mighty mountain rent asunder and forming a chasm which is a valley of grandeur and beauty, through which flows the Colorado Grande.
